who we are

KREDIT.DE AG was founded in 2017 in Hameln, Lower Saxony. Today, the company also has offices in Hamburg and Dresden. KREDIT.DE operates across all sectors and offers tailor-made financing solutions for a wide range of requirements - from personal loans to building financing and strategic debt relief. As a training company, KREDIT.DE also gives young people across Germany the best possible start to their professional lives.
