who we are

KREDIT.DE operates across all sectors and offers tailor-made financing solutions for a wide range of requirements - from personal loans to building financing and strategic debt relief. Frequently asked questions and answers about KREDIT.DE

Honest financial advice only works on an equal footing. That's why we place great value on honesty and mutual respect. Below we answer some questions that customers often ask us.

  • What distinguishes KREDIT.DE from other credit brokers?

    We take care of your wishes and concerns personally. We do everything we can to find the right financing for your needs. That's why the conversation with KREDIT.DE always ends with a concrete solution proposal. We analyze the initial situation and give you 100 percent honest feedback. Perhaps a loan will help you reach your goal - but perhaps we have to take other paths.

  • How much does personal, individual advice from KREDIT.DE cost me?

    The advice and service from KREDIT.DE are completely free for you. If we successfully arrange a loan, we receive a commission from the lending banks. We use this income to cover our costs. That's why you never have to pay any consulting fees or make any kind of advance payment.

  • How does KREDIT.DE make money?

    KREDIT.DE is a credit broker that works independently with over 500 banks and financial institutions. We are not bound to the terms and conditions of individual banks, but choose the optimal loan offer based on individual requirements. If a loan agreement is concluded through our mediation, we receive a commission from the lending bank. The service and advice from KREDIT.DE remain completely free of charge for our customers.
